The President of India is Smt. Droupadi Murmu, who became the 15th President on July 25, 2022, serving as the head of state, the country's first citizen, and the Supreme Commander of the Indian Armed Forces. She previously served as the Governor of Jharkhand, and her presidency focuses on empowering marginalized communities, with her residence at Rashtrapati Bhavan in New Delhi.
